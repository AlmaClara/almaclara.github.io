About Dr. Darshana

Dr. Darshana Thakkar Trivedi

BPT, MPT, MIAP, GSCPT

Specialist in Neurology & Psychosomatic Disorders

With over 15 years of experience in physiotherapy, Dr. Darshana specializes in neurological rehabilitation and psychosomatic disorders. Her patient-centered approach combines evidence-based techniques with compassionate care to help patients achieve their maximum functional potential.

Dr. Darshana completed her Bachelor's and Master's in Physiotherapy from renowned institutions and has since worked with thousands of patients, helping them regain mobility and improve their quality of life.
